ABOUT ME

I’m someone who learns quickly, adapts naturally, and works with intent. Whether I’m joining a team or taking on a new challenge, I approach each situation with observation, reflection, and action. I believe in getting the job done with accuracy and focus while staying calm and steady, even under pressure. In both my professional and personal life, I tend to be the person others turn to when they need help; whether that means solving a problem or just being a shoulder to lean on.

I’m ambitious, inspired by the people around me, and always looking for ways to grow. If I don’t know something yet, I’ll teach myself-I’ve never had an issue learning quickly and adapting on the spot. That said, I don’t believe in “faking it till I make it”; if there’s a new skill I want to engage in, I won’t pretend to know it, I will put in the effort to learn it properly through research, practice, and by seeking guidance if required.

Currently, I’m an aspiring front-end designer/developer with a strong foundation in HTML and CSS, and JavaScript, and a growing expertise in Python and React. As of now I’m passionate about creating responsive, visually appealing experiences for users. My journey started with my father and eventually my older brother being in the industry as full stack software engineers for over 15, years, and now me, a continuation of a literal bloodline of developers. When I was 14, I learned Python v2, building a very crude text-based adventure game. Fast-forward to 2020, Covid-19 happens, and I'm graduating from high school. Being stuck at home with no school or job, I decide to continue my passion and pick up coding again only this time I take a different approach; web design. Since then, I have been building up my experiences with the same languages. I have experience building structured and accessible designs and am eager to apply my problem-solving skills to real-world projects. Eventually, my dream is to move into mobile app development.

I value being open-minded, dependable, and self-aware. Furthermore, I work hard—even when things get difficult or tiring—and I know when to take things seriously and when to enjoy the process. I’m excited to bring these qualities with me wherever I go.
